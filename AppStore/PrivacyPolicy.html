Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ReverseWorldGo is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services.

1.1 User-Generated Content

  • Mirror Photos & Videos: Photos and videos you capture with mirror effects are stored locally on your device.
  • Reversed Text: Text content you create or reverse is stored locally.
  • Journal Entries: Your reverse journal entries are stored locally.

1.2 Usage Data

  • App Usage: We track basic app usage (features used, time spent) locally to improve user experience.
  • Statistics: Your usage statistics and achievement progress are stored locally.

1.3 Camera & Microphone Data

  • Camera: Camera access is used solely for mirror photo/video capture. Your camera feed is never stored or transmitted.
  • Microphone: Microphone access is used for voice input. Audio is processed locally and never transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is processed as follows:

  • Local Storage: All user content (photos, videos, text) is stored exclusively on your device.
  • On-Device Processing: All text reversal, voice processing, and mirror effects are performed locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on external servers.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Local-Only Storage: All sensitive user data is stored locally on your device, not on external servers.
  • Secure Deletion: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app. All local data will be permanently removed.
  • No Account Required: ReverseWorldGo does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.

4. In-App Purchases

ReverseWorldGo offers premium subscriptions through the App Store:

  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We never collect your payment information.
  • Purchase History: Your subscription status is managed by Apple and stored locally on your device.
  • Restoration: You can restore purchases using the "Restore Purchase" feature in the app.

5. Camera & Microphone Permissions

ReverseWorldGo uses your device's camera and microphone:

  • Camera Permission: Used only for mirror photo/video capture. Your camera feed is never recorded or transmitted.
  • Microphone Permission: Used only for voice input. Audio is processed locally and never transmitted.
  • Photo Library Permission: Used to save your mirror creations to your photo library.

6. Children's Privacy

ReverseWorldGo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all data stored locally by opening the app.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data.
  • Privacy Controls: You can manage all privacy settings within the app's Settings screen.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
